About Us

V-square Realtors is a real estate company based in Theni, which was started with the sole notion of transforming dreams of people into reality. Ever since our formation, we have serviced customers with the intention of getting them excellent properties at affordable cost. We offer a wide range of services from DTCP and REEA approved housing plots, house construction with interior works, land purchase consulting, farm land purchase and turnkey projects.

Our knowledgeable team at V-square Realtors ensures that our company maintains its high standards in terms of planning, execution and delivering projects to our customers. We have tie ups with leading banks to ensure our consumers dreams come into reality with proper financial advice and planning. With a strong and wide spread digital presence, we want to spread our wings across Tamilnadu and beyond to showcase the excellence in our services.
